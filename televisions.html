Television Energy Consumption

Modern LED televisions use significantly less energy than older plasma TVs. On average:

  • LED TV (40–50 inch): 60–100 kWh per year
  • Plasma TV (50 inch): 200–400 kWh per year

Energy-efficient TVs can save households up to $100 per year on electricity bills.
